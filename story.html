Our Story

What We're About

Real Food Crafted with Expertise

We believe in honoring the craft of cooking — everything is made from scratch, inspired by the seasons, and rooted in technique.

Founder: Lilo Felix

Chef and creator, Lilo Felix envisioned a platform that restores integrity to the culinary process and reconnects chefs with real ingredients and real people.

COO / Partners

With backgrounds in hospitality, wellness, and regenerative agriculture, our partners are mission-driven professionals committed to culinary and operational excellence.

Experience & Background

We bring together a network of chefs and collaborators whose collective resumes span private dining, sustainable sourcing, and cultural cuisine from around the globe.

Why We're Doing This

Beneficial Service to All Involved

Our model works because it centers real value: better meals, direct relationships, and pride of work for chefs, farmers, and clients alike.

Keep Food Production Local, Not Industrial

We reject the soulless scale of mass production. Every ingredient is sourced locally to support clean, resilient food systems.

Real Local Farmers Supported Directly

We don't just buy local — we collaborate directly with farmers, ensuring transparency, sustainability, and fair compensation.

Local Chefs Given Opportunity

We provide chefs with real creative freedom, career stability, and a chance to showcase their craft within their own communities.
